Thank You for Stopping By...

My name is Ophelia Julien, author of Saving Jake and the Bridgeton Park Cemetery series, among others. I think of myself as a writer of Young Adult Supernatural fiction, but am delighted to find that my audience has expanded to all ages. In keeping with my Young Adult writer identity, I am available for author visits to schools, libraries, Young Authors programs, writing groups and other such gatherings. I am also happy to come to book clubs, writers groups, and paranormal events. I love hearing from other paranormal enthusiasts, so feel free to contact me via email through this website, or on Facebook. And thanks for reading!
